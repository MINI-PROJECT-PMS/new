             A placement management system is a software application
designed to streamline the process of recruiting and hiring
employees for a company or organization. The system can automate
tasks such as job postings, resume screening, interview scheduling,
and candidate selection, making the hiring process faster and
more efficient.
            The placement management system enables recruiters and
HR managers toefficiently manage the recruitment process, by
tracking job openings, collecting and analyzing resumes, shortlisting
candidates,and scheduling interviews. The system can also provide
an applicant tracking system (ATS) to automate and simplify the
recruitment process.
